About this prototype
This is a browser-based mock-up, not the final iOS/Android app. Sunrise/sunset/azimuth values are real (SunCalc library). Compass heading uses your device's real orientation sensor where your browser/OS permits it. AR camera view is a simplified overlay, not true AR tracking.
